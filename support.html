Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my game progress?

You can reset your game progress by going to the Settings menu in the app and selecting "Reset Progress".

Is Greenify available on Android?

Currently, Greenify is only available on iOS devices. We may expand to Android in the future.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues with the app, please email us with details about the problem, including your device model and iOS version.

Will there be more games added in the future?

Yes! We plan to add more environmental education games in future updates. Stay tuned!

Is the app suitable for children?

Absolutely! Greenify is designed to be educational and engaging for users of all ages, including children.
